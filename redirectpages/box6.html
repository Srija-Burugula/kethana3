PRODUCTS FROM RICE STRAW

Bioplastic

Rice straw can be used as a feedstock for producing bioplastics, offering a sustainable alternative to traditional plastics derived from fossil fuels. These bioplastics can be used in packaging, disposable items, and other applications.

Cosmetics

Extracts from rice straw can be used in cosmetic products due to their potential skin benefits. These extracts may have antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and moisturizing properties, making them suitable for skincare formulations.

Fabrics

Rice straw fibers can be processed and used to make textiles and fabrics. These eco-friendly fabrics can be used in clothing, upholstery, and other textile applications, offering a sustainable alternative to conventional fabrics.
