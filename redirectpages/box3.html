PRODUCTS FROM FRUIT PEELS

Compost

Vegetable and fruit wastes can be composted to create nutrient-rich soil for gardening and agriculture.

Food Products

Wastes can be processed into juices and smoothies, providing a source of nutrition and reducing waste.Some fruit and vegetable wastes can be processed into snacks, such as chips or crisps.

Health Supplements

Wastes containing vitamins and minerals can be processed into supplements.Certain food wastes can be fermented to produce probiotic supplements.Wastes like fruit and vegetable peels, seeds, and pulps can be dried and processed into protein powders.

Natural Dyes

Red cabbage waste can be used to create blue, purple, and green dyes.Beetroot waste can be used to create a range of pink and red shades.The outer skins of onions can be used to create a range of colors from yellow to brown.

Sanitary Pads

Sanitary pads made from banana fibers are a sustainable and eco-friendly alternative to traditional pads. Banana fibers are biodegradable and have excellent absorbent properties, making them ideal for menstrual products
