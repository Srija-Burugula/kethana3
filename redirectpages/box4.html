PRODUCTS FROM HUSK

Biochar

Husks, when subjected to pyrolysis, can be turned into biochar, a type of charcoal used in soil amendment and carbon sequestration.

Biofuel

Rice husks and coconut husks can be used to produce biofuels such as bioethanol and biogas through processes like gasification and fermentation.

Fiber

Coconut husks are processed to extract coir fiber, which is used to make ropes, mats, and other products. Rice husks can also be used to produce fiber.

Fiber boards

Coconut husks can be processed into fiberboard, a type of engineered wood product used in furniture and construction.

Pots

Coir pots are biodegradable pots made from coconut husks. These pots are used in gardening and nursery operations.
