PRODUCTS FROM DRY LEAVES

Composts

Dry leaves can be composted to create nutrient-rich organic compost, which can be used as a natural fertilizer for plants and gardens.

Mulch

Dry leaves can be shredded and used as mulch to help retain moisture in the soil, suppress weeds, and improve soil health.

Insulation Material

Dry leaves can be used as an insulating material in construction, helping to regulate temperature and reduce energy consumption.

Animal Bedding

Dry leaves can be used as bedding for animals, providing a comfortable and absorbent surface for them.

Decorative Crafts

Dry leaves can be used in various craft projects and decorations, such as leaf art, wreaths, and table centerpieces.
