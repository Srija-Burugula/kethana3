PRODUCTS FROM DRY LEAVES

Biodegradable Packaging

Bagasse can be molded into packaging materials, such as trays, plates, bowls, and containers, which are compostable and eco-friendly..

Tableware

Bagasse can be used to make disposable cutlery, cups, and plates, providing a sustainable alternative to traditional plastic or paper products.

Paper Products

C Bagasse fibers can be used to produce paper products, including copy paper, notebooks, and tissue paper, offering a more environmentally friendly option compared to conventional paper made from trees.

Construction

Convert bagasse fibers into self-binding fibers using water and machines. Mold into desired shapes for packaging or tableware.

Animal feed

Bagasse can be processed and used as a component in animal feed, providing a nutritious and fibrous supplement for livestock.
